Our approach
The Lipi vs. Nambikke framework
Lipi
Inscribed evidence
vs.
Nambikke
Oral tradition & local belief

Significant knowledge was lost through invasions, natural disasters, and time. We recover it by travelling to sites, recording what ASI guides and local elders carry in memory, and cross-referencing those living traditions against physical inscriptions. Neither source alone tells the whole story.

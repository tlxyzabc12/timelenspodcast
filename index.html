Our approach

The Lipi vs. Nambikke framework

Lipi
Inscribed evidence
Physical inscriptions, epigraphy, and archaeological record
vs.
Nambikke
Oral tradition & local belief
What ASI guides, archakas, and village elders carry in memory

Significant knowledge was lost through invasions, natural disasters, and time. We recover it by travelling to sites, recording what people carry in living memory, and cross-referencing those traditions against physical inscriptions. Neither source alone tells the whole story.
